Lesson 16 | Overview Impacts of Globalisation on Business

1.5.1
Globalisation: opportunities and threats of globalisation for businesses.

These quantitative skills are also addressed in this lesson:

Calculation

Calculations in a business context:

  • percentage changes.

In this lesson, students will learn to:

  • understand the opportunities globalisation creates for businesses
  • understand the threats globalisation creates for businesses.

Students should already be familiar with the meaning of globalisation, its key features and the reasons for it (see Lesson 15).

Subject vocabulary

  • competitive advantage: being able to gain a favourable position against rival companies
  • hostile takeover: a company being taken over by another when it does not want to be
  • stakeholder: an individual or group with an interest in the operation of a business

General vocabulary

  • auction site: online website where products are sold to the person who offers the most money for them
  • migration: the movement of people from one country to another

Pearson Edexcel International GCSE (9–1) Student Book: pp. 68–71


In this lesson, students will each need a calculator.

  • Some students may feel that companies will experience either opportunities or threats from globalisation. Be clear when explaining both that there will be a range of impacts on any business and these will not be uni-directional, but will instead be complex.
